About Me

My passions are programming and fitness. If you notice the connection, it's all about exercise. Exercising the mind and body, to be exact, but, let's focusing more on the programming side. I very much enjoy building projects during my leisure. I love the satisfaction of solving and completing the required task--that I'm given or give to myself. That bright rush as though you have placed the final piece of a jigsaw puzzle excites me! When did it all begin? Let's have a moment for nostaglia. Before facebook; before Mark Zuckerberg; there was Myspace. And on this platform, in an effort to be unique, users had to implement and manipulate code to design their profile page. Sure, I didn't formulate the code myself, still, copy/pasting was enough to spark the curiosity of "what else can I do?" Thank you Tom Anderson (co-founder of Myspace) for showing me the creative side of coding and being my first friend on Myspace!
